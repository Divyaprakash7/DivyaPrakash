Job Tracker 2026

Job Tracker

I built a Job Tracker website to help simplify the chaos of job hunting. Instead of juggling spreadsheets, sticky notes, and scattered emails, this app lets users manage their entire job search in one place. Users can add job applications, track their status (applied, interviewing, offered, rejected), set follow-up reminders, and store important details like company name, role, application date, and notes — all in a clean, intuitive interface. The goal was to make job searching feel less overwhelming and more organized, giving users a clear picture of where they stand across multiple applications at a glance. Whether you're applying to a handful of roles or dozens, the tracker keeps everything structured and easy to update.
